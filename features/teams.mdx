Teams let you collaborate on workflows with other people. Create a shared workspace, invite members, and control who can view or edit your automations.

## Creating a Team Workspace

Team workspaces are organizations where multiple users can collaborate. Your personal workspace is always available, and you can create additional team workspaces depending on your plan.

## Inviting Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open team settings">
    Navigate to your team workspace and open the team settings panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite by email">
    Enter the email address of the person you want to invite and select their role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a role">
    Assign one of three permission levels to the new member.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Roles and Permissions

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Admin** | Full access. Can manage members, billing, and all workflows. |
| **Write** | Can create and edit workflows, but cannot manage team settings. |
| **Read** | View only access to workflows and their execution history. |

## Plan Limits

| Plan | Max Workspaces | Members per Workspace |
|------|---------------|----------------------|
| Free | 1 (personal only) | 2 invites |
| Pro | Up to 3 | Unlimited |
| Team | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | Unlimited |
