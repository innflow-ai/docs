## Available Integrations

Innflow connects with popular services to extend your workflow capabilities.

## Setting Up Integrations

1. Go to **Settings > Integrations**
2. Click **Connect** next to the service
3. Authorize Innflow to access your account
4. The integration is now available in your workflows

## Authentication

Integrations use OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication. Your credentials are encrypted and stored securely.

To disconnect an integration:
1. Go to **Settings > Integrations**
2. Click **Disconnect** next to the service
3. Confirm the disconnection
